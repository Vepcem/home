The V Meeting of Materials Science and Engineering Researchers (EPCEM) aims to create an environment that provides an unparalleled opportunity for scientific discussions of great importance, thus contributing to the development and innovation in the area of ​​Materials. The V EPCEM will be held from August 22 to 26, 2022 in a virtual format, which will solidify itself as an event of international, national, and regional scope, both for its regularity and for the ease of reaching a larger audience due to the virtual format. Thus, the aim is to facilitate the exchange between the various research groups from the most different countries and Brazilian regions. A larger audience than in previous events is expected, with the participation of professors and students from the most diverse programs. There will be a presentation of student works that will be evaluated by a quality scientific committee, where the best works will be awarded to encourage the dissemination of science. In addition to the possibility of student interaction with renowned national and international researchers. It will be a pleasure to have you with us at our event.
